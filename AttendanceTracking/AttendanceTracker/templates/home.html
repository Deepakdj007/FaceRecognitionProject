About us

Automated attendance tracking project by Team 07 S8 CSE alpha, RSET Kakkanad.



One of the most perplexing problems faced by modern schools and colleges today concerns the matter of taking attendance. Due to the amount of time and effort it takes to maintain the attendance of a class, a majority of teachers nowadays are very much tired of the present system of calling out student names before class to take the attendance.

The present system wastes so much valuable time and effort of the teachers that could well be used for something more productive. Thus, we felt a need to seek for a solution for this problem.

We propose an effective, accurate and automated attendance tracking system that uses computer vision facial recognition algorithms that could track, mark and review attendance within seconds.


Know more about us.

Our Objective

The prime objective of our project is to develop an attendance tracker that uses a computer vision model to recognize students from images. It is aimed for an academic environment where faculties find it difficult to take attendance manually. It is designed to be quick, accurate, reliable and to automate almost all the administrative tasks that are related to taking attendance and dealing with reports.

Learn More

What do we offer?

The features that we provide include options to take attendance, view attendance, edit attendance, send notifications to students with an attendance percentage of under 75, and generate reports.
